Police Contract Reform

Often, police abuses are not addressed because the union contract negotiated with the city has clauses that prevent investigation, discipline and termination for abuse of power.

Help pass a ballot measure to stop this:

"Any portion of a police contract that limits the ability to investigate, discipline or terminate law enforcement officers for abuse of power is null and void."

If we receive enough signatures by July 3rd, we can possibly make the ballot in November!
